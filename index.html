Who we are

Know Our Story Easily

UCF is a trusted leader in global trade and logistics, specializing in the export of premium fruits, vegetables, frozen products, and crude & refined oil from Egypt. With a strong network of reliable suppliers, we ensure the availability of fresh, high-quality produce at competitive prices, even during peak seasons. Our expertise extends beyond exports—we provide comprehensive ocean and land freight solutions, ensuring seamless and efficient transport across international markets.

Serving major destinations including the EU, CIS, and the Middle East, we are committed to excellence, sustainability, and customer satisfaction. Through strategic partnerships, innovative logistics solutions, and eco-friendly practices, we simplify global trade while delivering exceptional value to our clients.

The Process of Working with Us

Working with us is a straightforward and transparent journey designed to maximize efficiency and ensure your satisfaction at every step.

  • Leave a request

    Begin your journey by submitting a request. Provide details about your needs, and our team will promptly begin tailoring our logistics solutions to fit your specific requirements.

  • Cost calculation

    Receive a detailed breakdown of costs. Our team assesses the best shipping routes and methods to offer you an accurate and competitive pricing estimate, ensuring transparency and cost-efficiency.

  • Signing of a contract

    Formalize your commitment with us through the signing of a contract. This step ensures that all terms are clearly defined and mutually agreed upon, securing a solid foundation for our partnership and your peace of mind.

  • Execution of works

    Watch as we turn plans into action. Our skilled team ensures that every aspect of the logistics process is handled with precision and efficiency, from loading to shipment, ensuring your goods reach their destination on schedule.
